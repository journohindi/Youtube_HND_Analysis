Execution Directives

Critical

Pacing & Clickbait Disparity

Observation: "4300 धर्मों में से..." pulled 5.7M impressions but had a catastrophic 11.33% APV.

Action Protocol

Never pair a broad-appeal hook with niche, slow-paced delivery. Front-load the core philosophical payoff within the first 60 seconds. Enforce aggressive visual pattern interrupts every 8-12 seconds.

Critical

Narrative Intro Collapse

Observation: Narrative videos ("किताब लिखते-लिखते...") suffer sub-10% APV.

Action Protocol

Delete traditional chronological storytelling. Start the edit at the climax. Open the first 10 seconds with the subject's moment of crisis, then hard-cut to the conversion. Fulfill the psychological promise instantly.

High

Empty Shorts Equity

Observation: Shorts like "नमाज़ की जमाअत..." get 2M views but require ~1,964 views to gain 1 sub.

Action Protocol

Stop treating Shorts as standalone commodities. Engineer a funnel: pin a high-contrast comment directing to long-form, or implement a rapid 2-second visual CTA at the exact emotional peak.
